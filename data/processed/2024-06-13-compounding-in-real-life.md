# ​ Compounding isn't always about money

May 2024 was the best month for us at wariCrew — on YouTube and Instagram both.

* On YouTube, we reached our highest ever watch-time (total duration for which people have watched our content).
* It was 911,000+ hours!!
* We added 165,000+ subscribers in the month.
* It took us 3.5yrs to get to our first 165K subscribers :))

![](/img/missing-image.png)

* n Instagram, our content reached the highest number of accounts ever - 30Mn!
* We added 187,000+ followers in the month.
* It took us 3.9yrs to get to our first 187K followers.

![](/img/missing-image.png)

s is compounding in real life!   
Everything that we do, every day, compounds — investing, reading, working out, eating right, writing, having a routine, etc.

And it applies to the 'bad' things as well — complaining, criticising, worrying, procrastinating, etc.

**The decision of whether it will compound or not is not yours!   
The choice of what to compound is.

# Results of last week's survey

**Which aspect of your career do you wish school/college taught you about?**

Here are the results:

* Money management/Investing - the highest rated - with over 60% stating this.
* Followed by communication and public speaking - over 45%.
* Followed by how to manage one's emotions - over 30%.
* Followed by how to manage office politics - over 25%.
* Followed by taxes - over 20%!

My response:  
I wish school/college taught us how to manage our money well once we start earning. And not rely so much on our salary to grow our wealth!
