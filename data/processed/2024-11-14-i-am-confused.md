# ​ Stop lying to yourself

I received an email this morning.   
8 years of work experience.   
Has 2 job offers.  
Confused which one to choose.

In his words, both offers are the same.  
Same CTC.  
Same brand value.  
Same stability.  
Same location.

Here is what I replied:  
​*If both are the same, then you would have just tossed a coin, no?   
Clearly, both are not the same.*

**#1 Stop lying to yourself.**​  
It is the biggest reason why we make terrible decisions.  
We are dishonest with our own selves.

**#2 Answer honestly, which option do you prefer?  
​**Do not think of what you should do or what is the right thing to do.  
Ask yourself - what do you WANT TO DO?

**#3 Given your preferred option, what’s the hesitation?**​  
What is troubling you?  
What are your fears?  
What are your concerns?  
Solve for those.  
That is the most important thing.  
Everything else is a distraction.

**Solve for what comes in your way of doing what you want to do!

## 1 new thing I learnt this week

There is a path between Europe and the USA called the Jet Stream, which is like a highway for planes. Almost all planes travelling from Europe to the USA, and vice versa, fly on this patch itself because of the strong air currents, which give the airplanes a drift. The winds are from NY to Europe, and that's why flying from NY to (say) London takes a shorter duration than flying from London to NY.
