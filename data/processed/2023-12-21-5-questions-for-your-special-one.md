**​  
Questions for your special one**

1/ If you had to introduce me to someone completely new, what would you say about me?

2/ Is there anything about me that you did not like before, but have started liking now?

3/ Was there a fear inside you, regarding us, that you do not have anymore?

4/ What is the most difficult thing in this relationship?

5/ If, for whatever reason, we won't be able to talk for a long time, what is that one thing you want me to remember always?

As the year ends, this can be a great way to wind up time with your loved ones.
