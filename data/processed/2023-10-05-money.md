**Views about money**

1/  
The true role of money is NOT to buy things.   
The true role of money is to grant us freedom - over our time, our choices, and the way in which we lead our lives.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/dlSX0)**​

​

2/  
Wealthy people have it figured out:  
They’re wealthy because they don’t build their lives around a salary.   
They use their time and ideas to create multiple income streams.   
They work for money. But they also put their money to work.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/oFdf9)**​

​

3/  
Professional degrees help you earn a living.  
Financial literacy helps you build wealth.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/M641u)**​

​

4/  
Ultimately, the biggest asset you have is yourself.   
Your mind. Your body.   
Your education. Your well-being.   
Eat well. Exercise. Learn.   
It opens doors to new opportunities.  
Invest in yourself.   
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/c3aik)**​

​

5/   
Before you let emotions in, let your rational side take over.  
Run the numbers first.  
Because emotions will eventually come in, irrespective.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/l05ha)**​

​

6/   
If you’re just “saving”, it’s a random number.  
If you don’t hit it, you don’t care.   
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/gb1lG)**​

​

7/

Saving = Needs minus Ego.  
Your needs: Rent, groceries, electricity, and other can’t-avoid-it essential bills.  
Enter your ego: The new iPhone that you want, the Starbucks coffee you must have every day to look cool, those cool sneakers, the high-end gym membership, etc.  
When you take out your ego expenses from what you're left with after your needs, that's saving.

​

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Nds2!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F70e6f4ce-ca23-4d2c-9da3-ce06ab45cfe4_1024x1024.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Nds2!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F70e6f4ce-ca23-4d2c-9da3-ce06ab45cfe4_1024x1024.jpeg)

​

8/   
DON’T go through life focusing only on a savings mindset.  
There’s a limit to how much you can save.   
But remember there’s no limit on how much you can earn.  
Keep finding ways to increase your income.  
That will help you build wealth much faster than saving will.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/k0vp8)**​

​

9/  
True financial independence comes:  
When income from your investments pays for your needs and expenses. For life!   
When you work because you want to, not because you have to.   
When you have money to live well, even without the 9 to 5 grind.  
That’s financial freedom.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/qYrFA)**​

​

10/   
Risk can’t be eliminated.   
It can only be understood and managed.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/4ec8z)**​

​

11/  
Financially smart people take loans, even if they can afford it, to save money.   
Financially weak people take loans, knowing that they can't afford it, to spend money!  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/ciwM7)**​

​

12/  
Everybody can create value.   
Everybody can build wealth.   
It doesn’t have to come at anybody's expense.   
There can be multiple winners.  
In the game of money. In the game of life.   
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/6aE35)**​

​

13/  
Don’t forget that the only thing you actually wanted was happiness - that’s the reason you were chasing money in the first place!  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/Evw2M)**​

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G2_i!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F23379c4d-4d16-4cbc-a626-6407e6b00af8_1024x1024.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!G2_i!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F23379c4d-4d16-4cbc-a626-6407e6b00af8_1024x1024.jpeg)

​

​

14/  
Money will not change you.   
It will reveal you.   
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/89uho)**​

​

15/  
A lot of things that we think take courage actually just take money.   
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/O3P2G)**​

​

16/  
In a status-driven rat race, you can never win.   
There’ll always be someone who has bigger, shinier, and more expensive things than you.  
Spare yourself from this.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/3oLaz)**​

​

17/  
Having less doesn't make you poor.  
Always wanting more does.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/4Ae5d)**​

​

18/  
Saving is hard.  
Being broke is harder.  
Learning about investments is hard.  
Suffering from bad investments is harder.  
Paying off debt is hard.  
Letting your debt compound is harder.

Choose your hard.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/16a09)**​

​

19/  
Whenever you’re in doubt, bet on the only thing you can bet on.   
Yourself.   
Life will show you a way.   
It will all be okay.  
You will be ok.  
​**[(Share on x)](https://ctt.ac/nkdv6)**​
