6 Habits to Change Your Life**

1/ Sleep at the same time every day. Wake up at the same time every day. For 7+ hours.

2/ Eat healthy, and no more than 3 times a day. Avoid sugar/fried.

3/ Read for 15 minutes every day. Not online.

4/ Exercise/walk at least 5 times a week. For 15 minutes at least.

5/ Do nothing for 5 minutes every day - no phone, book, music, person, or anything else.

6/ Schedule distraction time during the day for social media. Do not consume it whenever you wish to.

Which of these 6 are you going to start in 2024?

# ​ 📕 Book I am reading right now

This week, I picked up **[Same as Ever: Timeless Lessons on Risk, Opportunity and Living a Good Life](https://amzn.to/47lJYHa)** by Morgan Housel (author of Psychology of Money).   
It builds on an interesting premise - What will be the same ten years from now? What will be the same one hundred years from now? Because Morgan believes that knowledge of the things that never change is more useful, and more important than an uncertain prediction of an unknowable future.

I am enjoying the read. I am sure you will too.
