# ​ 3 realisations from last week's team offsite

Our team works remotely.   
So, we go for 4 offsites a year!   
We just returned from our 14th offsite; 4 days in Shillong filled with travel, food, laughter, and relaxation!

In our team meeting, I shared 3 key points for everyone - not just businesses, but individuals too.

1/  
This year marks our 5th anniversary.   
We began in March 2020 with 6 people, aiming to create accessible, helpful content and courses.   
In 5 years, we’ve:  
- Gained 15M+ social media followers  
- Taught 440,000+ students  
- Generated 85Cr+ revenue at 25% EBITDA margin  
- Distributed 1.97Cr for 397 students’ education  
- Shared 2.5Cr as team profit

What worked before won’t work moving forward.   
Processes, technology, and team members will change. Embrace it to stay relevant and purpose-driven.

For you:  
Adapt; what worked once may not again.   
Change to grow.

2/  
We’re no longer small.   
We love our lighthearted, child-like spirit, but our actions and mistakes now impact many.

We bear responsibility and must act thoughtfully — vet partnerships, and measure moves — while staying humble.

For you:   
Growth is inevitable.   
Your words and actions will be judged.   
Take it seriously, wear it proudly, and let your inner child shine.

3/  
After 5 years, some team members have been in roles for 2–5 years.   
Stagnation is possible, and while the company accepts meeting expectations, you shouldn’t.   
Keep growing and avoid comfort.   
Use people and tech for leverage, and your time to build yourself.

For you:   
Winners rent knowledge, not time.

I’m excited for [WebVeda](https://example.com/blog/2025-03-20-we-went-on-our-14th-offsite-last-week/www.webveda.com)’s next 5 years.   
Hope you are for yours too! :)
