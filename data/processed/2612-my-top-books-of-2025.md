# My top books of 2025

I completed 24 books in 2025.   
  
While much lesser than usual, this is not a metric I care about.

I enjoy my books a lot and give each book as much time as I enjoy reading it.   
With some, it is a few minutes (before dropping them) and for some, it’s weeks (because the book makes me think as much as it makes me read).

From all the books I read in 2025, there are 6 books I would rate 5/5:

[![Image](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qRTN!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa13da3b0-c913-4f0b-87e3-2dbe7e9af25a_2048x2048.jpeg "Image")](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!qRTN!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fa13da3b0-c913-4f0b-87e3-2dbe7e9af25a_2048x2048.jpeg)

**[The Body](https://amzn.to/3QIPBsk)** is one of the best books I have read, not just this year, but overall. Goes deep into understanding the human body and the miracle that it is.

**[Molecule of More](https://amzn.to/3UxtyGM)** made me understand dopamine in a way I have never before. And being able to relate it to so many of our daily reactions and actions.

**[Steal Like an Artist](https://amzn.to/3CFRRgD)** is an incredibly simple yet powerful book. A must-read for everyone. Lifts the pressure off most people on how to create good quality work.   
The book also served as a massive inspiration for my book, “Beyond The Syllabus”, which I wrote for teenagers.

**[Born A Crime](https://amzn.to/4ldNCcc)** was the best autobiography of the year. Written in a near immaculate manner, Trevor’s journey from South Africa to becoming one of the top comedians in the world was mesmerizing.

**[Jugalbandi](https://amzn.to/4pm9Axf)** was the book of the year for me. I learnt so much about the history of modern Indian politics. Must read for anyone interested in the same.

**[Think Like a Freak](https://amzn.to/4kqrOKr)** is a super fun book to read. It challenges the way we think and forces the reader to consider an alternative.

At my end, I released 2 books this year, both of which went on to become bestsellers, for which I am immensely grateful to all of you.

**[Build An Epic Career](https://amzn.to/4j8ROuu)** was my book to the 24-year-old confused Ankur, who was certain that his career was over after dropping out of his PhD. The lessons I learnt along the way are what I have shared.

**[Beyond The Syllabus](https://amzn.to/4axEDkI)** was a bold attempt to speak to teenagers, an audience I rarely speak to. The book aims to share with them what school/college will rarely teach them.

Happy reading in 2026, to all of you :))

# Kids are winning laptops and iPads!

We have now crossed 100,000 challenges played on **[India Genius Challenge (IGC)](https://www.indiageniuschallenge.com/?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=igc_main)** - a national search for the brightest kids in the country.   
9,000+ kids are playing online, across 800 cities and 700 schools in the country!   
  
Here’s how it works:  
- Kids play daily 5-min challenges on general knowledge and logical reasoning, absolutely free of cost.  
- Daily national winners get earphones, Weekly national winners get smartwatches, and monthly national winners get laptops and iPads!   
- The top performers will be invited to Delhi for the National Finals, where the IGC winner will get a Rs. 10L scholarship

**Check out IGC on [this link](https://www.indiageniuschallenge.com/?utm_source=newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=igc_main).**
