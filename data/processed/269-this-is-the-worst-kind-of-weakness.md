# The worst trait in any human

Every human is a mix of good and bad parts.   
We all hope that the sum of our good parts is greater than the sum of our bad parts.

But there is one bad part, in my opinion, that outweighs every other good or bad part.   
Losing your temper.

You see, most good or bad things are internal.   
If you are honest, hard working, disciplined, truthful, and committed - these good things make YOU a better person.   
Others get to see the impact of it; the result of these good things.   
  
Similarly, most bad things are also internal.   
Jealousy, hatred, indiscipline, dishonesty, and lack of integrity.   
They affect you more than they affect anyone else.   
Others simply get to see the resultant human because of these bad traits.

Losing your temper is one of those rare bad things that is as much external as it is internal.   
  
It is different from anger (which is something we all feel within).   
Because it takes a very weak person to allow that anger to become shouting.   
  
To be so consumed by the anger, that the only way you have left to channelize it is to direct it at someone else.   
By shouting.

Shouting tries to disguise your weakness as being in control or command.   
Shouting tries to justify that you have been wronged.  
Shouting tries to convince you that you are standing for your rights.

But none of this is true.   
Shouting simply shows that you reached a point where you couldn’t internally process the situation.   
And externalising it was the only mechanism left for you to calm down.

**If you lose your temper, you still have a long way to go.**
