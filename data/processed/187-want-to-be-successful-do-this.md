# Surest way to be successful

There is one necessary condition to be successful.   
The ability to work with other people.

No one can win on their own.   
We all need others, and we all stand on the shoulders of others.

But there is a nuance that most of us miss.   
It is not just any person who can make you win.   
You need the right kind of person.

Someone who is smart, hardworking, committed, driven and of high integrity.   
Such people are extremely rare to find.   
But they do exist.   
Such people can help you grow, can help you scale, can help you think objectively and can help you become a better version of yourself.

So when successful people find such people, they do something that sets them up for success.   
**They are extremely generous towards such people.**

Generous with their time.   
Generous with their money.   
Generous with their help.   
Generous with their advice.

And that’s the big mistake most of us make.   
Instead of asking *“Who will help me get ahead?”,* we should ask, *“Who can I help today that I genuinely want to see win?”*

And that makes all the difference!

> **The surest way to win in life is to be generous with hardworking people.

## 1 new thing I learnt this week

Urine (pee) comes from blood and not from what you drink!

When you drink water or other fluids, they are absorbed into your bloodstream through your digestive system.  
Your kidneys constantly filter this blood, removing waste products (like urea, creatinine) and excess water or salts.  
The filtered waste and water become urine, which is then sent to the bladder and eventually excreted.

So, in short:  
You drink → it enters your bloodstream → kidneys filter the blood → you pee.
