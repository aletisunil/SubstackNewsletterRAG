# ‚Äã Here is what young people do not have

I received an email this morning.   
She earns well, has a stable job, is well-qualified, and has been investing for over 5 years.   
Her investment corpus is 25 lakhs, which is impressive.   
But her email mentioned a common, naive goal:   
‚Äã*"I want to reach 1 crore in the next 3-4 years."*

In personal finance, compounding is key.   
‚Äã  
For those unaware, here‚Äôs a quick explanation:   
Start with 100 rupees and earn 10% annually.   
You might think the amount you make every year stays the same, but it doesn‚Äôt.   
Year one: 10% of 100 is 10, making it 110.   
Year two: 10% of 110 is 11, totalling 121 (110 + 11).   
Year three: 10% of 121 is 12.1.   
Every year, you earn more than the last, even at the same rate.   
Over time, this growth compounds, resulting in what is called an exponential curve.

‚Äã

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!i-Dm!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F151b6d16-3a70-4fab-a1e9-4642a5c26a17_744x400.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!i-Dm!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F151b6d16-3a70-4fab-a1e9-4642a5c26a17_744x400.jpeg)

‚Äã

This post isn‚Äôt about compounding ‚Äî most of you know it.   
It‚Äôs about a problem with the youth: You understand compounding, but haven‚Äôt experienced it.   
That‚Äôs not your fault because you haven't had what is the most important ingredient of compounding:  
Time!   
If you‚Äôre in your 20s or 30s, you haven‚Äôt seen 15-20 years of compounding.   
This leads to a desire to "Get rich fast", like in the email.

The only quick way to wealth is the lottery.   
Nearly everything else takes time and relies on compounding.   
But without seeing it work, you might doubt it ‚Äî leading to risky shortcuts or scams promising instant returns.

Now, you have two options:   
1. Ignore compounding and chase quick riches  
2. Trust it and build wealth steadily.

What will you choose?
