# OMG - he is so rich!

Someone asked on Twitter - **What was the pinnacle of wealth to you as a child?**

I was reminded of a story from school.   
Featuring my classmate, whom we shall call A.

This was 1997.   
I was 17.   
11th Grade.   
Bryan Adams released an album called *18 til I Die*.  
And I LOVE Bryan Adams.   
I wanted to hear this album, on demand (yeah, no Spotify during those days!)

The only way to hear the album on demand was through something called CDs (or compact discs).   
Shiny silver discs that required a CD player to play.   
For those who were born recently, this is what I am talking about…

[![High Fidelity CD Ripper Player, Automatic Ripping Drive Converts CDs to WAV Files with Automatic Transcription,Copying CDs to Any Storage Device via PC,for Home, TV, Mac,PC,Digital Player,Tablet](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Wkvp!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc73a344f-4051-49bb-a30c-b659e6c632a0_1500x1265.jpeg "High Fidelity CD Ripper Player, Automatic Ripping Drive Converts CDs to WAV Files with Automatic Transcription,Copying CDs to Any Storage Device via PC,for Home, TV, Mac,PC,Digital Player,Tablet")](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Wkvp!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fc73a344f-4051-49bb-a30c-b659e6c632a0_1500x1265.jpeg)

My cousin had a CD player.   
BUT I didn’t have the album’s CD.   
They cost a lot of money.

I knew that A has the CD.   
So I asked him if I could borrow his CD for a few days.   
He is a nice guy. He said yes.

The next day, I go to school, excited about A handing me the Bryan Adams CD of 18 till I die!  
He took it out of his bag.   
The CD cover with the CD inside.

[![Bryan Adams - 18 'til I Die. | Köp på Tradera (692113502)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!V1Nk!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca023e3d-37c4-4c11-a841-452e31bc35e2_900x675.jpeg "Bryan Adams - 18 'til I Die. | Köp på Tradera (692113502)")](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!V1Nk!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fca023e3d-37c4-4c11-a841-452e31bc35e2_900x675.jpeg)

  
The CD was freezing cold.   
Like, really cold!   
I was confused.

“A, why is this CD cold?”  
”What do you mean by cold?” he replied.   
”This CD cover is cold, like you kept it in the fridge or something.”  
”Ohhhh haha! It is the AC in my bedroom, bro!”

*He has a bedroom?   
He has an AC in the bedroom?  
He has an AC in the bedroom that runs all night?  
He has an AC in the bedroom that runs all night — so much so that he travelled all the way from home to school, and the CD is still cold?*

HE IS SO RICH!!!

That, for me, was the pinnacle of wealth, growing up.   
A bedroom, with an AC, that runs all night!

Crazy!

## 1 new thing I learnt this week

The stethoscope was invented in 1816 when French physician, Rene Laennec, wanted to listen to the heartbeat of an overweight female patient, but was reluctant to put his ear on her chest.   
Remembering that sound could be heard through a solid object, he rolled up a sheet of paper, placing one end against her chest and the other in his ear.
