# The thing you do not want in life will be what you get most in life

Saw a video that reminded me of the Black Coffee Theory, which I had read a long time back. Here’s how it goes:

You go to a coffee shop.   
Because you want to have a normal milk coffee.   
You reach the counter, and the guy at the counter asks, “So what can I get you today?”  
You reply, “I don’t want a black coffee.”

The guy is confused and asks again, “Umm, ok. But what do you want?”  
You reply, “I don’t know. All I know is that I do not want a black coffee.”

It’s a busy day for the guy.   
He goes back to completing all the orders.   
By the time he returns to your order, he can’t quite remember what it was.   
All he remembers is you constantly mentioning “black coffee.”

So after 5 minutes, a black coffee lands on your table!

That is how most of us live our lives.   
We keep telling the universe what we don’t want.   
What we fear.   
What we want to avoid.   
What we don’t want to be.   
What we want to run away from.

And all that the universe hears - is that thing.   
So it gives us that thing!   
That very thing we did not want in our lives.

**If you keep repeating what you do not want in life, be prepared to receive it the most in your life.***[(Share on X)](https://ctt.ac/1d1ZP)*

To win, you need to be super specific about what you want in life.   
”I want a normal milk coffee.”

That’s it!
