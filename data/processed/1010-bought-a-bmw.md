# Not me. Someone I know :)

This post is to celebrate a thought.   
A thought that comes really late, but eventually comes.   
A thought which even if we knew would come later, doesn’t help us when we are down and dejected.

The thought that “Life eventually works out.”

Shaurya, aged 25, joined my team 5 years ago.   
He was still in college.   
Doing BBA.   
After having attempted the IIT-JEE and gotten rejected.   
This made him feel like an absolute failure, especially since his dad was from IIT!  
To top this, he doesn’t redeem himself by doing engineering.   
Instead, he loses his mind and does a BBA degree!

He weighed 110+ kgs.  
Felt like he let down his parents.  
Hated his college.  
Was convinced his life won’t work out!

Shaurya bought a BMW last week.   
Not for himself.   
For his dad - as a retirement gift.   
A 55L retirement gift!

[![Image](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hFRM!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd91bbb0-9953-4943-a2b6-a9ab91f34412_3072x4096.jpeg "Image")](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!hFRM!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbd91bbb0-9953-4943-a2b6-a9ab91f34412_3072x4096.jpeg)

He earns 7 figures every month.   
The down payment of the car would be less than 5% of his net worth.  
The EMI would be less than 7% of his monthly salary.  
Plus, he is his fittest self now!

It took him just 5 years, for his life to work out.

But go back to the day he didn’t make it to IIT.   
Go back to the day when he realized he was in an uninspiring college.   
Go back to the day when he would look at his body and perhaps feel ashamed.   
On those days, knowing that ‘Life will eventually work out’ would NOT have helped.

And that’s the irony of life.

Life eventually works out.   
But it doesn’t help to know that, when you are dejected.   
When you have failed.

Here is what helped me:  
KEEP MOVING!

If we all believe that life will eventually work out, the only thing we can do is to keep moving.   
Not wallow in our pain.   
Not feel like a victim.   
Not blame others.   
Just keep moving!

Shaurya moved.   
Life eventually worked out.

And it will for you as well.   
You got this :)
