# Compounding isn’t just about money

A lot of us understand the concept of compounding.   
At least theoretically.   
We understand that even at the same rate of growth, we earn more and more every year, because the base on which the growth rate applies keeps growing.

But a lot of people assume that compounding is a money concept.   
Because it is usually applied in that context.   
If you were to invest 1Cr today and let it grow at 12% every year, then at the end of 20 years, you will have ~10Cr!  
Wow!

But compounding isn’t limited to just money.  
It spreads across all dimensions of life.

This is the power of compounding in real life:

**Public Speaking**  
The first time I spoke on stage, I was in class 7.   
It was a terrible experience.  
I could barely speak.   
My friends made fun of me.   
Shaken by it, I swore never to step on stage again.

Until class 11, when my class teacher pushed me to fill in for one of the class 12 farewell hosts who fell sick.  
She saw in me something that I did not see in myself.   
That gave me the confidence to keep pursuing it.  
I continued to practise my public speaking through debating in college.

After 20 years of compounding, I have spoken at 450+ events.  
I am invited by the top colleges and companies across the world and paid a lot of money to speak.   
The same kid who promised himself he would never set foot on stage again.

**Content Creation**  
I started my YouTube channel in August 2017.  
For 3 years, I posted 1 video every week without fail.   
Using my phone and a tripod.   
All by myself.   
After 3 years, in 2020, I had 20k subscribers.

I kept making videos.  
Today, I am in the top 1000 YouTube channels in the world with 6 million+ subscribers.   

Habits are the hardest to build, early on.  
But once they begin to work, they will change your orbit.

> The pro is the amateur who showed up every day!
