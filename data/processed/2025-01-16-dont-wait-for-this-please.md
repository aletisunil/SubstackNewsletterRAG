# ​ Don't wait for the tools

*"Ankur, your photos look awesome. Which phone do you use?"*

*"Ankur, your videos are so sharp. Which camera do you use?"*

*"Your voice sounds so clear and deep. Which mic do you use?"*

*"You have been meditating for so long without fail, which app do you use?"*

These DMs make me smile :))  
It's equivalent of asking someone, "Your book was wonderful. Which pen did you use?"

A lot of us are stuck in the wait for the right tools.  
The right camera, the right phone, the right laptop, the right course, the right book, and the right app.  
The right moment.

What we fail to recognize is that the tool is just that.  
A tool.

A tool doesn't replace the hard work.  
It elevates the hard work.  
​  
A tool doesn't improve the art.  
It refines the artistry.  
​  
A tool doesn't replace the skill.  
It enhances its application.

If you keep waiting for the right tool, you will never give your talent the chance to shine on its own merit.

**Share your art today.  
The tool will come!

# My fourth book is now launched

At the age of 24, I recall being super confused, scared, anxious, and worried about my career.   
I felt like a complete failure.

And every day, I receive emails and DMs with the same emotions.   
I do not know what to do.   
I am scared of letting down my parents.   
I am worried I will end up as a loser.

Much like money, we rarely have the right conversations growing up about careers.   
It is always about what we should become - a doctor, engineer, lawyer, CA!  
It is never about how to think about our career.

So I wrote my fourth book with the aim of helping that 24-year-old Ankur.   
And through that, helping you.
