**Decisions taken at the moment!**

I had a call with one of my mentees this morning.   
He is 3 years into his first job and is wondering if it's the right time to quit or if he should continue.   
I asked him what he wants to do.

"I don't know. If I stick to the job, I will gain expertise and build skills that will help. But I am not really happy here - so I am not sure if continuing here helps or not. Plus, I do not know what else I can do. What will be the right thing to do?"

I asked him again.  
"What do you want to do?"

"Oh, I don't know. The thing is, I haven't been very deliberate about my career until now. I did not plan for it. I just landed on it. I see my friends - like this girl I know who always wanted to be in academics and is pursuing that. She is so clear on what she should do. I am not."

I asked him one final time.  
"What do you want to do?"

"I have told you I do not know what I should do."

That right there is the problem.   
I am not asking you what you should do.   
I am not asking you what is the right thing to do.   
I am asking you - what do you WANT to do?   
​

​

Here is the deal about life.   
Early on - NO ONE knows what they should be doing.   
So you have two options - do what everyone else is telling you to do. Or do what you want to do.   
I think if you do what you want to do - you have a higher chance of success.

To get there, there are 3 questions that help.

1. **What are the things that I WANT to do?**​  
   These could be anything - do not put any constraints as yet. Do not think of whether you will get it, whether it's the right thing for the future, or whether you are qualified.   
   No constraints.   
   Just a list of all the things you want to do.
2. **What is stopping me?**​  
   This is where the constraints are listed.   
   It could be your capability, it could be your parent's permission, it could be lack of opportunities, or it could be the fact that it doesn't make enough money.  
   It could just be your mind tricking you that you can't do it.   
   Whatever is stopping you from doing it - figure that out.
3. **Which of these are in my control to fix?**​  
   THIS is the clincher.   
   This is where you transfer control - from the world to yourself.   
   ​**YOU ARE IN CHARGE.**   
   Not your circumstances, your family, or the world.   
   Which of these constraints are in your control to fix?  
   And you know what? You will realise that there are only so many things in your control. BUT if you were to take control of them - you will actually be ahead of most people in the same boat who want to do the same thing but never start.

**Just 3 questions**

1. **What are the things that I WANT to do?**
2. **What is stopping me?**
3. **Which of these are in my control to fix?**

**To take charge of your life!**

Try them today.

Finished [Stealing Fire: How Silicon Valley, the Navy SEALs, and Maverick Scientists Are Revolutionizing the Way We Live and Work](https://amzn.to/459Y2lx).

Started and finished **[Be You. Now!: Redefine Your Approach to Life,](https://amzn.to/44aFmBp)** by Sagar Makwana.  
Sagar was a student of our last course, "How to Instagram", and he was kind enough to present me his book on Convocation Day. I read it last week and it was a genuinely good read.   
The book resonated a lot with my approach towards life and I found myself nodding and smiling a lot more than I usually do.  
Delightful read - please pick it up as a self-help book that will truly help.

Must read for every tech or philosophy enthusiast.
