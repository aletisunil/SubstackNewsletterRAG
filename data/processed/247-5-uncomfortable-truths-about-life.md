# 5 uncomfortable truths about life

**Uncomfortable Truth 1:**Life has no purpose. ​   
We came from nothing.   
And once we are gone, there will be nothing.   
No one will remember us beyond a few years or decades.   
And yet we spend every second of our lives wondering what people think of us!

**Uncomfortable Truth 2:**   
Whatever you are feeling today will fade away. ​   
No matter how happy you are or how sad you are, you are not going to remember how happy or sad you were.   
Our emotions and feelings are temporary. We think of them as permanent.

**Uncomfortable Truth 3:**   
It takes effort to find love. ​   
It takes effort to feel fulfilled.   
It takes effort to be fit.   
It takes effort to be happy.   
Which is why most of us do not have love, fulfillment, happiness or fitness in our lives.

**Uncomfortable Truth 4:**   
We always have choices. ​   
No one is ever stuck.   
We are just scared to make those choices.

**Uncomfortable Truth 5:**   
We struggle to love ourselves because we attach our self-worth to what others think of us. ​  
But we can’t control what others think of us.

**Which one of these hits the hardest?**

## 1 new thing I learnt this week

It is impossible to hum while holding your nose.   
When you hum, air needs to pass through your nose to create the sound. If you pinch your nose shut, you block that airflow.

I know you are trying this right now, hahah!  
Close your nose tightly, ok? That will stop the hum.
