2. I am surprised at the high use of credit cards in the older segments. Clearly a very niche audience in this newsletter.
3. The use of credit has been increasing significantly in India, in general.

**My response:**​  
I rarely use a credit card now. Used to a lot, but with UPI, it's so much easier.
