**THE SHOCKING TRUTH ABOUT FEAR**

---

Here is a quiz question.

When we are born, are we born with any fears? *Think about it before reading further.*

Yes - we are born with 2 innate fears   
​ *(you read the answer before answering it in your head. Haha! I know you did! Liar!)*

Just 2 fears - the fear of falling. And the fear of loud sounds.

When we are born, we don't fear anything else! NOTHING!

No fear of drowning, no fear of snakes or tigers tigers, no fear of cutting ourselves, no fear of sharp objects.

And, by that same measure...

...no fear of failing, no fear of the future, no fear of judgement, no fear of boredom, no fear of people, no fear of monsters/darkness/ghosts

Every fear that you have today, besides falling and loud sounds - was taught to you.

Think about this for a moment

EVERY fear that you have today, was something that your world introduced you to.

Remember the time your parents or grandparents were narrating that story and then came a tiger in it?

And they went, "TIGERRRRRRRRR" - converting their hands into the tiger's paws, making a fierce sound and trying to get to get scared?

Remember the time your elder sister/brother/cousin hid in the dark and as soon as you came in the room, they pounced on you shouting, "BHOOOOTTTT" *(ghost for Hindi!)?*

Remember the time you got poor marks in your school and your parents were upset, possibly shouted at you, maybe even hit you?

Remember the time you approached that cute boy/girl in your class and they laughed back at you?

Remember the time you stood on stage, speaking to an audience for the first time, and after that your friends made fun of you?

​

Well, you get the point.

Every fear that we are paralyzed by - was taught to us. We weren't born with it.

And you know what that means?

We can undo the teaching!

If you have forgotten all your history or math or physics lessons from school, then I guarantee you that you can forget your fears too.

It just requires you to not give them any importance!

​

Because fears serve just one purpose. It is our body's way of telling us that there is potential danger.

Don't go up to that tiger, you might die.   
Don't cross the road, you might get hit.   
Don't speak to that person, you might get rejected.   
Don't speak the truth, you might be shouted at.

And over time our body accepts these fears as defense mechanisms.

But if you were to ever reach a point where you tell your mind, "I know why I am feeling fearful," then suddenly the body goes, *"Oh awesome. My job is done. I wanted to bring this fear to alert this body. It seems they are already alerted. Cool! Let's go back, my dear fear. We are not needed!"*

And just like that, the fear recedes.

It is almost magical. I speak from experience.

Try it out, will you?

**Don't fight your fears. Acknowledge them.**

**RESULT OF LAST WEEK'S POLL (SUPER INTERESTING - YOU HAVE TO READ THIS)**

---

Last week I asked all of you

Which one would you choose and why?

1. Get 7.5 Crore rupees (1 million dollars) right now
2. Get 5 lakh rupees (6,600 dollars) every month for the rest of your life

This was a trick poll - because it was NOT a poll. Instead a quiz question, with a correct answer (unlike the polls in the past with no right answer!)

The right answer to this question is - you take the 7.5 Crores right now.

BUT

60% chose the 2nd option of 5 lakhs per month!

I am not surprised, because most of those who selected that option are young in their 20's and have unfortunately not been trained in personal finance as yet.

​

There are 2 concepts you need to know about

1. Compounding
2. Inflation

When you take 7.5 Crores, you can invest all of that money. And earn an interest. FOREVER.

Even if you earned 10% interest on this amount (which frankly is quite easy - most stock Mutual Funds give 12-15% returns and even Debt funds give 8-10%), you will earn 75L per year. After paying taxes this will come to 60L.

Which means you will get 5L a month (or 60L a year) AND will still have the 7.5 Crores intact!

​

Then comes inflation.

Money loses value every year - by something called inflation (a video is coming up on this). Which means 5L today is NOT equal to 5L after 10 years.

Most of you said - oh I am 20 and I will die at say 80, so for the next 60 years I will get 5L every month which means I will get 30 Crores. Hence I will select the 2nd option of 5L per month.

No!

You won't get 30 Crores - you will get much lesser. Because there is inflation. Every year the value of our money decreases because things get expensive (which is what is inflation).

See how this works?

Let me know if you have questions on this.
