# Who is secure?

If there is one trait that instantly attracts me to people, it is their sense of security.   
They know who they are.   
They are comfortable with who they are.   
They walk, talk, and express with an air of confidence that is appealing and magnetic.

How does one get to this sense of self-assurance and security?   
I can tell you it is not their looks or physique.   
It is not their skill or talent.   
It is not their values or upbringing.   
It is not that they have never done anything wrong.   
It is not that they know they can never make a mistake.

None of these traits makes someone secure.

What makes someone secure is that they are constantly working on themselves.

These individuals have made mistakes, but have learnt from them.   
They have committed errors, but have fixed them.   
They have been ignorant, but have learnt what they needed to.   
They have been misguided, but worked hard to find the truth.

These individuals know that the person they are today is far better than the person they were yesterday.   
That is why they are so comfortable admitting their mistakes, sharing them openly, and acknowledging their gaps.   
Because they know they are not that person anymore.   
They have buried that person with intense hard work, discipline, and a fervent desire to become a better version of themselves.

The most secure person you will find is not the one who never made a mistake.   
Instead, it’s the one who is forever growing.   
Forever leaving their past self behind.   
Forever discovering a new self.
